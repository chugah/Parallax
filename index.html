My Studio

The WORK

I’ve been fortunate enough to have worked with some of the best talent. Artists like to work with me because I am a collaborator at heart throughout the songwriting and recording process. Whether it's arranging, mastering, or everything in between, I strive to capture the essence of who you are as an artist and what you are hoping to accomplish through your music.

A few clients: The Scorpions, Kottak Attack, Michael Schenker, Blaze Bailey, George Lynch, Killinger, Producer Julie Larson Stevie Wonder, Etta James, Chaka Kahn, Elton John, Steely Dan, Janet Jackson, KD Lang, Anne Lennox, Sarah McLachlan, Lindsey Buckinham, Bjork, Producer Gary Sinclaire, Chris Layton (Stevie Ray Vauhan), NBC, ABC, CBS, FOX SPORTS, and many more.
